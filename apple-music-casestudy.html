01 / THE CONTEXT

The Problem: While Apple Music offers superior audio quality, its interface has often been criticized for being "sterile" compared to the vibrant community of Spotify. Users find the navigation clunky, often requiring 3-4 taps just to access their library.

The Goal: To create an interface that feels "alive." I wanted to merge the album art with the UI, creating a fluid, glassmorphic experience that adapts to the mood of the song.

02 / VISUAL IDENTITY

I refined the Apple Design System to be darker and more immersive. The color palette extracts key tones from album art dynamically.
